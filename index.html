Child Safety Standards

Ambar Dating — Preventing child sexual abuse & exploitation (CSAE/CSAM)

Overview

Ambar Dating maintains a strict zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM). This document explains our prevention measures, reporting process, moderation practices, cooperation with authorities, and contact details.

Age Requirements

  • Our service is intended only for users 18 years and older.
  • We do not knowingly permit minors to create accounts. Accounts suspected to be underage are investigated and removed.
  • We employ age-gating, user attestations at signup, and additional verification when suspicious activity is detected.

Prevention & Detection

To prevent and detect CSAE/CSAM we use a layered approach combining automated tools and human review:

  • Automated scanning of images and file uploads using industry-standard hashing and detection tools.
  • AI-assisted content moderation and pattern detection for grooming or sexual solicitation behavior.
  • Human moderation team that reviews flagged content and escalations 24/7.
  • Rate limits, upload restrictions, and content filters to reduce exposure risk.

Reporting & Takedown

Users may report suspected CSAM or abusive behavior directly within the app via the in-app report flow. When we receive a report:

  • We assess reports promptly and remove confirmed violating content immediately.
  • We preserve evidence and metadata (timestamps, user IDs, IPs, etc.) necessary for investigations.
  • We retain logs according to legal requirements and our retention policy to support law enforcement requests.

Cooperation with Authorities

Ambar Dating complies with applicable laws about reporting CSAM to relevant national and regional authorities. When required, we share necessary account and content information with law enforcement under lawful requests.

Moderation & Sanctions

  • Confirmed policy violators are permanently banned.
  • We maintain an internal escalation process for serious or repeated offenses, including immediate account suspension and referral to law enforcement.
